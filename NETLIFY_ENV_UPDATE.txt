NETLIFY ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES UPDATE:

After Railway deployment completes:

1. Go to Netlify Dashboard
2. Navigate to: Sites → premium-cinema-booking → Site Settings → Environment Variables
3. Add/Update these variables:

REACT_APP_API_URL=https://your-railway-app-name.railway.app/api
REACT_APP_USE_MOCK=false

4. Click "Deploy site" to redeploy with new backend connection

REPLACE "your-railway-app-name" with your actual Railway app URL!
